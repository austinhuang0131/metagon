About Lydia itself

  • Isn't the bot called Metagon before? Yes, you are right. It was served as a fork of the Discord bot. However the Discord-side developer left Discord and threw the project to another two people. You can read the somehow-detailed explanation here.
  • You said you are not associated with them...? I am not associated with them de facto, however, they gave me permission to use the brand.
  • Why did you move out from Discord? Very simple. Discord bot market has been expanded too fast. And there are problems in the community. Actually, the problem is the same as in United States: Separation between normal citizens and elites. I think you will understand that.
  • Does hosting Lydia costs? I didn't even use a cent in Lydia: My VPS is free (Openshift Redhat v2, no longer available for registration), my APIs are free, and I am using an Uptime Robot to ping my VPS every 5 minutes so it will be technically 24/7.
